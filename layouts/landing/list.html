Xinyi Zhang (张新一)

Associate Professor, School of Business, Sun Yat-sen University

zhangxy599@mail.sysu.edu.cn

Ph.D. in Management (Accounting), School of Economics and Management, Tsinghua University. His research interests include accounting and financial regulation, data assets, and artificial intelligence and capital markets.

Work Experience

  • Associate Professor, School of Business, Sun Yat-sen University, February 2025 – present.
  • Assistant Professor, School of Business, Sun Yat-sen University, September 2020 – January 2025.

Education

  • Ph.D. in Management (Accounting), School of Economics and Management, Tsinghua University, June 2020.
  • Visiting Scholar, Marshall School of Business, University of Southern California, January 2018 – June 2018.
  • Master of Accounting, School of Economics and Management, Tsinghua University, July 2015.
  • Bachelor of Management in Accounting, School of Management, Xiamen University, July 2013.
