Xinyi Zhang is an Associate Professor at the School of Business, Sun Yat-sen University. He received his Ph.D. in Management (Accounting) from the School of Economics and Management, Tsinghua University in June 2020, his Master of Accounting degree from Tsinghua University in July 2015, and his Bachelor of Management degree in Accounting from the School of Management, Xiamen University in July 2013.

His main research interests include accounting and financial regulation, data assets, and artificial intelligence and capital markets. His research examines issues at the intersection of accounting, corporate finance, capital market regulation, corporate governance, managerial incentives, information disclosure, leverage regulation, convertible bonds, and emerging data- and AI-related capital market questions.

His published work includes research in Management Science.

He has served as Principal Investigator for research projects including the National Natural Science Foundation of China Youth Project, the Guangzhou Basic and Applied Basic Research Program "Qihang" Project, and the Sun Yat-sen University Fundamental Research Funds for Central Universities Youth Faculty Cultivation Project. He also serves as Academic Director of the Student Academic Development Center at the School of Business, Sun Yat-sen University, and as Editorial Office Director of China Journal of Accounting Research (CJAR).
